What I actually do

Every project starts from a business question, not a dataset.

Revenue decomposition

Revenue Analytics

E-commerce Growth Decomposition

PythonSQLPandas
Problem
Revenue was growing, but nobody could say whether it was coming from more customers, more frequent purchases, or bigger baskets.
Approach
Decomposed revenue into Users × Frequency × AOV across time periods and customer cohorts to isolate the real growth driver.
Result
A repeatable framework that turns "revenue is up" into "here's exactly why, and here's the lever to pull next."

Segmentation + LLM

AI Marketing Agent

PythonGPT / LLMRFM
Problem
Customer segments existed on paper, but nobody had time to translate them into actual marketing actions per group.
Approach
Built RFM segmentation, then connected segments to an LLM pipeline that drafts targeted marketing actions automatically per group.
Result
A working bridge between "who are our customers" and "what should we actually send them" — without a human doing it manually every time.
