Segmentation + Forecasting

Customer Segmentation & Revenue Forecasting

PythonK-MeansProphetTableau
Problem
A UK online retailer had transaction data but no way to tell which customers mattered most, or where sales were headed next quarter.
Approach
Built RFM-based customer segments ("Champions", "At-Risk", etc.) with K-Means clustering, then forecast future sales with the Prophet model — packaged into a Tableau dashboard and a stakeholder-ready presentation.
Result
A segmentation + forecasting pair that tells the business who to retain now and how much revenue to expect next.
